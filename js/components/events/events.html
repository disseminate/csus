Events

Throughout the year, CSUS runs a number of events for students, bridging the gap between the academic world and social life. In past years, we have organized events both on and off campus, including:

  • Laser tag
  • Bowling night
  • Pub nights
  • Exam prep and review
By far, our most popular event is our annual Academic Colloquium, where students gather for an evening of talks presented by the department's leading researchers. Be sure to keep in touch with us to find out what we'll be doing next!
